Weapon Categories & Playstyles

In Valorant, your weapon is your greatest ally, with a diverse arsenal of guns designed for every playstyle. From precise one-taps to rapid-fire sprays, mastering the right weapon can make the difference between victory and defeat. Weapons are categorized into Sidearms, SMGs, Rifles, Sniper Rifles, Shotguns, and Heavy Weapons, each with distinct strengths and weaknesses.
